## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Can't find enough directories
**Solution:** Search "[niche] directories," "[city] business directories," "[industry] listings"

### Issue: Directories require verification
**Solution:** Use phone verification, email verification, or postal mail

### Issue: Backlink opportunities are hard to reach
**Solution:** Start with Tier 3 (easier), build to Tier 1 (harder)

### Issue: Citation data inconsistencies
**Solution:** Use exact same name, address, phone across all directories
