# Skill: rankrentbiz-competitor-analysis

## Overview
Analyze top competitors in your niche to identify ranking opportunities, content gaps, and weaknesses you can exploit to outrank them organically.

**Time Saved:** 1-2 hours per site
**Complexity:** Medium
**Repeatable:** Yes (for all 30 sites)

---

## What This Skill Does

Provides:
- ✅ Competitor overview (domain authority, traffic, keywords)
- ✅ SEO metrics analysis (rankings, backlinks, citations)
- ✅ Content analysis (pages, blog, schema markup)
- ✅ Weakness identification (missing content, poor SEO, no schema)
- ✅ Ranking opportunities (long-tail keywords, local gaps)
- ✅ Recommended strategy (how to outrank them)

---

## Prerequisites

Before using this skill, you need:
- [ ] Niche name (e.g., "Backflow Prevention Testing")
- [ ] 3-5 top competitor URLs
- [ ] Understanding of your target market
- [ ] Firecrawl MCP access (for web scraping)

---

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Identify Top Competitors (10 minutes)

**Search for:**
- Google search: "[niche] [city]" (e.g., "backflow testing Toronto")
- Look at top 5 organic results
- Identify 3-5 strongest competitors

**Example competitors for Backflow Testing:**
- dryerventwizardgta.ca (local competitor)
- National franchise sites
- Local plumber/contractor sites offering the service

**Document:**
- Competitor name
- URL
- Type (local, national, franchise)
- Apparent strengths

---

### Step 2: Scrape Competitor Sites (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Use Firecrawl to scrape the following competitor URLs:
1. [URL 1]
2. [URL 2]
3. [URL 3]

Extract:
- All pages and URLs
- Title tags and meta descriptions
- H1 and H2 headings
- Schema markup (LocalBusiness, FAQPage, etc.)
- Internal links
- External links
- Blog articles (if any)
- Contact information
- Service descriptions"

**What you'll receive:**
- Complete competitor site structure
- All content extracted
- Ready for analysis

---

### Step 3: Analyze SEO Metrics (20 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Analyze SEO metrics for each competitor:
- Domain Authority (DA) estimate
- Estimated monthly traffic
- Keywords they rank for (top 20)
- Backlink count (estimate)
- Citation count (estimate)
- Page speed (Core Web Vitals)
- Mobile friendliness
- Schema markup implementation"

**What you'll receive:**
- SEO metrics comparison table
- Strengths and weaknesses per competitor
- Ranking difficulty assessment

---

### Step 4: Content Analysis (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Analyze competitor content:
- Number of pages (total)
- Number of blog articles
- Location pages (how many cities/areas)
- Service pages (how many services)
- FAQ sections (yes/no, how many questions)
- Pricing transparency (yes/no)
- Schema markup types used
- Content depth (average word count)
- Internal linking strategy"

**What you'll receive:**
- Content comparison table
- Content gap analysis
- Opportunity identification

---

### Step 5: Identify Weaknesses (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Identify competitor weaknesses:
- Missing location pages (which cities?)
- Missing schema markup (which types?)
- No FAQ section (opportunity)
- No blog content (opportunity)
- No pricing transparency (opportunity)
- Poor mobile optimization
- Slow page speed
- Weak internal linking
- No local authority signals
- Outdated content"

**What you'll receive:**
- Weakness list per competitor
- Opportunity scoring
- Quick wins identified

---

### Step 6: Identify Ranking Opportunities (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Identify ranking opportunities:
- Long-tail keywords competitors don't rank for
- Local keywords (city-specific)
- Question-based keywords (FAQ targets)
- Informational keywords (blog targets)
- Service-specific keywords
- Comparison keywords
- Problem-solution keywords

For each opportunity:
- Keyword phrase
- Search volume (estimate)
- Ranking difficulty (low/medium/high)
- Our advantage (why we can rank)"

**What you'll receive:**
- Ranked list of opportunities
- Quick wins (easy to rank)
- Medium-term targets (3-6 months)
- Long-term targets (6+ months)

---

### Step 7: Create Competitive Analysis Document (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Create a comprehensive competitive analysis document with:
1. Executive Summary (1 paragraph)
2. Competitor Overview (table: name, URL, DA, traffic, strengths)
3. SEO Metrics Comparison (table: DA, traffic, keywords, backlinks, citations)
4. Content Analysis (table: pages, blog, location pages, FAQ, pricing)
5. Weakness Analysis (table: weakness, impact, our advantage)
6. Ranking Opportunities (table: keyword, volume, difficulty, priority)
7. Recommended Strategy (3-5 paragraphs on how to outrank them)
8. Quick Wins (top 5 easiest wins)
9. Timeline (3-month, 6-month, 12-month targets)"

**What you'll receive:**
- Professional competitive analysis document
- Ready to reference during site building
- Strategy for outranking competitors

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Can't find competitor URLs
**Solution:** Search "[niche] [city]" and look at top 5 organic results

### Issue: Competitor site won't scrape
**Solution:** Try different competitor or manually analyze their site

### Issue: Can't estimate DA or traffic
**Solution:** Use Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Moz estimates (or skip if unavailable)

### Issue: Competitors have no weaknesses
**Solution:** They likely do — look for missing location pages, schema, blog, etc.
