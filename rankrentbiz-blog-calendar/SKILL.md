# Skill: rankrentbiz-blog-calendar

## Overview
Create a strategic 24-article blog content calendar targeting long-tail keywords to drive organic traffic and establish authority in your niche.

**Time Saved:** 1-2 hours per site
**Complexity:** Medium
**Repeatable:** Yes (for all 30 sites)

---

## What This Skill Does

Provides:
- ✅ 24 article topics (6 months, 4 per month)
- ✅ Keyword research (search volume, difficulty)
- ✅ Content strategy (informational, problem-solution, comparison)
- ✅ Internal linking strategy (links to location pages, pricing, services)
- ✅ Publishing schedule (consistent cadence)
- ✅ SEO optimization checklist (per article)

---

## Prerequisites

Before using this skill, you need:
- [ ] Niche name (e.g., "Backflow Prevention Testing")
- [ ] Target cities (16 cities)
- [ ] Competitive analysis (from rankrentbiz-competitor-analysis skill)
- [ ] Ranking opportunities identified

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Identify Content Pillars (10 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Identify 6 content pillars for [niche]:
- Pillar 1: [Topic] (4 articles)
- Pillar 2: [Topic] (4 articles)
- Pillar 3: [Topic] (4 articles)
- Pillar 4: [Topic] (4 articles)
- Pillar 5: [Topic] (4 articles)
- Pillar 6: [Topic] (4 articles)

Each pillar should cover a different angle of the niche.

Example for Backflow Testing:
- Pillar 1: Fire Safety & Prevention (4 articles)
- Pillar 2: Technical & How-To (4 articles)
- Pillar 3: Maintenance & Costs (4 articles)
- Pillar 4: Code Compliance (4 articles)
- Pillar 5: Specific Issues (4 articles)
- Pillar 6: Advanced Topics (4 articles)"

**What you'll receive:**
- 6 content pillars
- Thematic organization
- Ready for article topics

---

### Step 2: Generate Article Topics (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Generate 24 article topics (4 per pillar) for [niche]:

Pillar 1: [Pillar Name]
- Article 1: [Topic]
- Article 2: [Topic]
- Article 3: [Topic]
- Article 4: [Topic]

[Repeat for Pillars 2-6]

Each topic should:
- Target a specific keyword
- Address a user intent
- Provide value
- Support ranking goals"

**What you'll receive:**
- 24 article topics
- Organized by pillar
- Ready for keyword research

---

### Step 3: Keyword Research (20 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Perform keyword research for 24 articles:

For each article topic:
- Primary keyword (e.g., 'dryer vent fires')
- Search volume (monthly searches)
- Ranking difficulty (low/medium/high)
- Related keywords (3-5 variations)
- User intent (informational/problem-solution/comparison)
- Local angle (if applicable)

Create a table with:
| Article | Primary Keyword | Volume | Difficulty | Intent | Local |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Article 1 | [keyword] | [volume] | [difficulty] | [intent] | [yes/no] |"

**What you'll receive:**
- Keyword research for all 24 articles
- Search volume estimates
- Difficulty assessments
- User intent mapping

---

### Step 4: Create Content Calendar (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Create a 6-month blog content calendar:

Month 1 (Weeks 1-4):
- Week 1: Article 1 - [Title] ([Keyword])
- Week 2: Article 2 - [Title] ([Keyword])
- Week 3: Article 3 - [Title] ([Keyword])
- Week 4: Article 4 - [Title] ([Keyword])

[Repeat for Months 2-6]

Include:
- Publishing date (specific week)
- Article title
- Primary keyword
- Content pillar
- Internal links (3-5 links per article)
- Expected traffic (estimate)"

**What you'll receive:**
- 6-month publishing schedule
- Article titles and keywords
- Internal linking plan
- Traffic projections

---

### Step 5: Internal Linking Strategy (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Create internal linking strategy for 24 articles:

For each article, identify:
- 3-5 internal links to:
  - Location pages (e.g., 'Backflow testing Toronto')
  - Service pages (e.g., 'Backflow device testing')
  - Pricing page
  - Other blog articles
  - Homepage

Example:
Article 1: 'Dryer Vent Fires: The #1 Home Fire Hazard'
- Link to: Homepage (anchor: 'professional dryer vent cleaning')
- Link to: Pricing page (anchor: 'dryer vent cleaning cost')
- Link to: Toronto location page (anchor: 'dryer vent cleaning Toronto')
- Link to: Article 3 (anchor: 'how to prevent dryer vent fires')"

**What you'll receive:**
- Internal linking map
- Anchor text suggestions
- Link distribution strategy

---

### Step 6: SEO Optimization Checklist (10 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Create an SEO optimization checklist for each article:

For every article, ensure:
- [ ] Title tag (50-60 characters, includes keyword)
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 characters, includes keyword)
- [ ] H1 tag (includes primary keyword)
- [ ] H2/H3 tags (includes related keywords)
- [ ] 3-5 internal links (to location pages, pricing, other articles)
- [ ] 1-2 external links (to authority sources)
- [ ] FAQ section (3-5 questions with answers)
- [ ] Schema markup (FAQPage schema)
- [ ] Image alt text (includes keyword)
- [ ] Word count (1,500-2,500 words)
- [ ] Readability (clear, concise, scannable)
- [ ] Call-to-action (link to pricing or contact form)"

**What you'll receive:**
- SEO checklist for all 24 articles
- Optimization guidelines
- Quality standards

---

### Step 7: Create Blog Calendar Document (15 minutes)

**Request to Manus Agent:**
"Create a comprehensive blog content calendar document with:

1. Executive Summary (overview of strategy)
2. Content Pillars (6 pillars, 4 articles each)
3. Article Topics (24 topics with keywords)
4. Keyword Research (table: keyword, volume, difficulty, intent)
5. Publishing Schedule (6-month calendar)
6. Internal Linking Strategy (links per article)
7. SEO Optimization Checklist (per-article checklist)
8. Traffic Projections (estimated traffic per month)
9. Success Metrics (KPIs to track)

Format as Markdown document."

**What you'll receive:**
- Professional blog calendar document
- Ready to reference during writing
- Complete strategy documented

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Can't find enough keywords
**Solution:** Use long-tail variations, question-based keywords, local keywords

### Issue: Keywords too competitive
**Solution:** Focus on long-tail (lower search volume, lower competition)

### Issue: Articles seem repetitive
**Solution:** Vary content types (how-to, problem-solution, comparison, listicle)

### Issue: Internal linking doesn't make sense
**Solution:** Link to relevant pages that support the article topic
